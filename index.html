About Me

Graphics Designer

I’m Jenna Mae Cambronero, a graphics designer driven by creativity, clean aesthetics, and the power of visual storytelling. I’m inspired by designs that communicate clearly and leave a lasting impression, and I enjoy transforming ideas into visuals that feel modern, expressive, and purposeful.

I graduated from City College of Calamba with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). I specialize in the field of Graphic Design, and I hold a National Certificate (NC III) in Visual Graphic Design, which strengthens my skills and professional foundation.
